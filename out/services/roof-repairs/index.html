Signs Your Roof Needs Repair

Don't ignore these warning signs - early detection prevents costly damage

Water Leaks

Stains on ceiling or walls indicating water intrusion

Missing Tiles

Broken, cracked, or missing roof tiles or shingles

Sagging Roof

Visible sagging or drooping areas on the roof surface

Granule Loss

Excessive granules in gutters or bare spots on roof

Daylight Visible

Light coming through roof boards in attic space

High Energy Bills

Sudden increase in heating or cooling costs

Mold & Mildew

Growth of mold or mildew in attic or on walls

Damaged Flashing

Rust, cracks, or gaps in roof flashing
